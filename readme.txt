== Description ==

Help Me Write for WordPress lets you share your ideas, supporters and stats from [Help Me Write](http://helpmewrite.co) in your WordPress sitebar!

== Installation ==

0. Register for a [Help Me Write](http://helpmewrite.co) account if you don't have one!
1. Upload `plugin-name.php` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Enter your [Help Me Write](http://helpmewrite.co) username in the 'Widgets' menu in WordPress - you can also customize the display of the widget there.

== Frequently asked questions ==

Q: Doesn't [Help Me Write](http://helpmewrite.co) replace my need for a WordPress blog?
A: No way! Help Me Write is a way for you to find out what ideas your readers would enjoy; we love WordPress and use it ourselves to publish our blog posts after testing the water on Help Me Write!

Q: Is [Help Me Write](http://helpmewrite.co) amazing?
A: Yes!

== How it works ==

For this widget to work it has to phone home to [Help Me Write's](http://helpmewrite.co) server to find your details. This doesn't mean anything to you, but the nice folks at WordPress asked us to mention it :)
